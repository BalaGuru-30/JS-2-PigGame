Rules 📜


Hi! Welcome to pig game 🐷.

1. The game is played between 2 players.

2. The first player to score 100 points (Total Score) wins the game.

3. If you wish to add your current point to the total score, press the hold button 📥. Once you press it your current turn ends and the next player begins to roll the dice 🎲.

4. Warning ⚠️ : When you roll the number 1, you lose all your current points (not Total points) and the next player gets their chance to play. So make sure to hold 📥 your points rather than losing all of it.

5. Hit the New Game button 🔄 to restart the game.

Have fun...! 😀
